How do people live and work?

This lens focuses on household composition and employment patterns. It highlights the rise of single-person households, the fall in average household size, and selected employment indicators that help explain changing forms of independent living. It characterises the type of housing demand generated by Lausanne's changing social fabric.

What does housing offer?
